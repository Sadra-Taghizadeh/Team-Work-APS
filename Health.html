          One of the most important way of having a healthy body is having a healthy nutrition.
        In simple words, nutrition is the process of obtaining or extracting the food elements required for the process of growth and development of a human body. 
        It is a biochemical procedure that includes ingestion, absorption, assimilation, biosynthesis, catabolism and further excretion.
        The study of nutrition is known as nutritional science.
